Our Story

Welcome to Ribediya KOI, an authentic Japanese restaurant in the heart of Sydney!!

Founded by Rie, Belle, and Diya, our name combines a personal touch with the koi fish, symbolizing prosperity and good fortune in Japanese culture. At Ribediya KOI, we bring together traditional Japanese culinary expertise crafted by our world-class chefs, combined with modern elements to create a unique dining experience.

Explore our dishes, learn about their cultural significance, make reservations, and stay updated on our latest specials. At Ribediya KOI, we bring the best of Japan to Sydney with a perfect balance of Japanese hospitality and Australian service.
